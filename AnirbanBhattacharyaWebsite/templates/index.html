About me

I was born on 30 August 2006. I was born in Kolkata, West Bengal. I was born in a middle-class family. My father was an employee in Indian Air Force. And my mother is a housewife. When I was 3-4 Years old we shifted to Ambala, Punjab because my father got posting over there. From there I started my education. I started my education at Air Force School and continued till the second class. Then my father got a posting again and we shifted to Lucknow. I continued my schooling in a convent school named St Mary Inter College. In this way, my life was going on those days. I was very happy. But my marks were very poor those days. My parents were also not satisfied with my marks. They tried to make me understand the importance of education in life. They often scolded me. But my marks were still not satisfactory. My marks were not satisfactory due to the absence of the parent guide. My father was always busy with his office works. Then my mother took responsibility for my studies. She took all my study responsibilities on her head. Then my marks started to improve only because of my mother. She is not highly educated but she has a piece of good knowledge to become a teacher. She taught me till the fifth class and I started scoring good marks. I was happy to see a sudden change in my marks. I was also very happy. After that, my father sent me to some personal tutions. Then once again my marks got deteriorated due to poor guidance. Then my father sent me to a grouped tuition classes, hoping that my marks will improve. And my father's wish came true. Again my marks started to increase. All this continued till ninth class. Then unfortunately a pandemic took place. The name of the pandemic was Covid-19(Coronavirus). Then everything got closed as it was a communicable disease. Then our school also got closed and online classes started. Then we were having a lot of time to spend at home. I asked my few friends that what they do at home at free time. They said that they do gaming at free time. But I was not like them. I started learning a computer language. I got my interest over there. I learned a computer language not completely but more than half. While I got drowned myself in such a way that I neglected my studies. Then as usual my marks also deteriorated in class 9 in the annual examination. My parents were also upset. Then I came to class 10. Then one month I drowned myself in programming. But after that, I started hard working. My dream is to become a software engineer. Now I am in class 10 and preparing for board examinations. I recently gave one part of my board exams. You might be shocked, but I am from the corona batch 2021 - 2022. So... So I prepared well in the term 1 exams but my marks are not upto my expectations. I scored just 71% aggregate. I was sad a little bit. But now I am working hard to do well in term 2 exams. Hope the exams will be good for me.
